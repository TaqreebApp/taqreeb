Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I reset my progress in the app?

A: You can reset your learning progress by going to Settings > User Profile > Reset Progress. Please note that this action cannot be undone.

Q: Is Taqreeb available for Android devices?

A: Currently, Taqreeb is only available for iOS devices. We're working on an Android version and will announce its release on our website and social media channels.

Q: Does Taqreeb require an internet connection?

A: Most of Taqreeb's core features work offline. However, audio pronunciations, additional verse examples, and synchronizing your progress across devices require an internet connection.

Q: How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

A: We welcome your feedback! Please email us at support@taqreebapp.com with details about bugs you encounter or features you'd like to see. Screenshots and screen recordings are extremely helpful when reporting issues.

Q: Are there plans to add more words beyond the 500 most frequent?

A: Yes! Our roadmap includes expanding the vocabulary database with additional word sets once you've mastered the core 500 words. Stay tuned for updates.
